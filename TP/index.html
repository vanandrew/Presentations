How does it work?

Non-linear Regression

The Synth Model

\[\begin{aligned} \mathbf{y} = f(\mathbf{M}_{\boldsymbol{\gamma}}\mathbf{B}\mathbf{F}\boldsymbol{\theta}; \boldsymbol{\phi}) + \boldsymbol{\epsilon} \\ \end{aligned} \]

\[\begin{aligned} \mathbf{y}: \text{Target EPI Image} \end{aligned} \]

\[\begin{aligned} f/\boldsymbol{\phi}: \text{Displacement Field/Parameterization} \end{aligned} \]

\[\begin{aligned} \mathbf{M}/\boldsymbol{\gamma}: \text{Affine Transform/Parameterization} \end{aligned} \]

\[\begin{aligned} \mathbf{B}: \text{Blur Kernel} \end{aligned} \]

\[\begin{aligned} \mathbf{F}: \text{Matrix RBF Decompositions T1/T2} \end{aligned} \]

\[\begin{aligned} \boldsymbol{\theta}: \text{Regression Weights} \end{aligned} \]

\[\begin{aligned} \boldsymbol{\epsilon}: \text{Gaussian Noise} \end{aligned} \]

Solve for \[\begin{aligned}\boldsymbol{\phi},\boldsymbol{\gamma},\text{ and }\boldsymbol{\theta}\end{aligned} \] simultaneously.
